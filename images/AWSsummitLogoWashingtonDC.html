AWS Summit Washington, DC | Free Public Sector Technology Conference | June 26-27, 2024

June 26-27, 2024 | WASHINGTON, DC

Join us at the Walter E. Washington Convention Center in Washington, DC on June 26-27, 2024. This no cost event is tailored for the public sector community. Attendees will engage in interactive learning, network among public sector industry leaders and AWS experts, engage with partners driving innovation, and so much more.

Add to your calendar »

Register now »

Interested, but can't make it in-person?

Join us virtually for a live broadcast of the keynote.

Mission advancement with cloud technology

Discover how Amazon Web Services (AWS) public sector customers are adapting to change and challenges and constructing innovative solutions in the cloud. Learn what it takes to drive culture change, digital transformation, and infrastructure modernization.
 
Customize your agenda as you choose from lecture-style presentations, interactive whiteboard case studies, peer-led discussions, solution building and gamified learning workshops, and a robust Expo hall. Engage in experiential learning led by AWS experts and partners and ask questions along the way.
 
AWS Summit Washington, DC is a complimentary event, so there is no cost to attend. If you are a business or IT decision-maker, gain insights from customer stories and use cases. If you are a technical leader, data scientist, developer, system administrator, solution architect, or engineer, tune in to our interactive content and step-by-step technical demos.
 
Have questions? Contact us.

Keynote hosted by Dave Levy, VP, Worldwide Public Sector, AWS.

Keynote

Time: 1.15 hours

Join Dave Levy, Vice President of AWS Worldwide Public Sector (WWPS), to learn more about the generative AI explosion and why the cloud is the foundation for this emerging technology. Discover how AWS customers of all shapes and sizes are unlocking generative AI’s potential to make the world safer and healthier, reimagine citizen services, and realize their boldest missions.

Why attend:

Leave with a firm understanding of how AWS empowers you to be at the forefront of the generative AI revolution while maintaining the resiliency, scalability, and security required in the public sector. Hear from featured customer speakers about their experiences, agility, and resilience with AWS, and gain insights into moving faster, increasing security, and reducing costs.
